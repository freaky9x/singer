SAMIR SHRESTHA

Samir Shrestha is an emerging artist in Nepal Music Industry. He has achieved a lot of fandom in the Nepali music industry. His songs like Chaar Deewar , Mero Man Ma , Thamana Haat has reached a great milestone .

BARTIKA EAM RAI

Bartika Eam Rai is a Nepali singer-songwriter based in New York City. In 2016, Rai burst onto the public consciousness with the release of her debut album, Bimbaakash. Rai has released two albums so far.Rai released her first single, Khai, on YouTube in January 2016 to widespread popularity.

SAJJAN RAJ VAIDHYA

Sajjan Raj Vaidhya is a Nepali singer, songwriter, guitarist, composer and producer. He released his debut single, "Aaja Matra", in 2014 and rose to prominence after the release of his singles "Hawaijahaj" (2016) and "Chitthi Bhitra" (2018).One of his most widely known singles is "Hataarindai, Bataasindai".Recently he has released his two new singles "Sasto Mutu" and "Ek Sharwanam" with the beginning of 2022.

YABESH THAPA

Yabesh Thapa is a popular Nepali singer and song writer. He is known for his husky vocals and compositions. Debuted with the hit song Lakhau Hajarau, Yabesh is one of popular singers of the generation. He is signed under Class X Production.

ANKITA PUN

Ankita Pun is singer/songwriter from Dang,Nepal . She started her Musical journey from 2018 covering Nepali songs on her YouTube. 20 year old Talent is already working on her debut album “ Maili “. She is now based at Butwal to continue her education and musical Journey.
